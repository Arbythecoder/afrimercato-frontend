## ğŸ”§ IMPLEMENTATION PLAN

### **Two-Tier Verification System** (Recommended)

#### Tier 1: **Express Verification (24 hours)** âš¡
For low-risk, established vendors:
- Registered businesses with valid licenses
- Vendors with verifiable business address
- Clear product categories (non-restricted)
- All documents uploaded and complete

**Process:**
1. Automated checks (instant):
   - Email verification
   - Phone OTP
   - Address validation
2. Admin review (within 24 hours):
   - Quick document check
   - Approve or flag for detailed review

#### Tier 2: **Standard Verification (48-72 hours)** ğŸ”
For all other vendors:
- New businesses or individuals
- Restricted product categories (meat, dairy, alcohol)
- Incomplete documentation
- Flagged by automated systems

**Process:**
1. Automated checks (instant)
2. Detailed admin review (24-48 hours):
   - Verify all documents
   - Check business registration
   - Validate licenses (if applicable)
   - Background check (optional)
3. Admin decision (24 hours):
   - Approve
   - Request more information
   - Reject with reason
