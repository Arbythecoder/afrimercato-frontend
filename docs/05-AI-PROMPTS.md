## ELEVENLABS VOICEOVER SCRIPTS

### Vendor Video - Complete Script for ElevenLabs

Copy this entire script and paste into ElevenLabs. Add pauses using `...` or `<break time="1s"/>` tags.

```
Welcome to Afrimercato – Africa's growing marketplace connecting vendors like you with customers who love fresh, quality products. In the next few minutes, we'll show you everything you need to know to start selling and growing your business.

<break time="1s"/>

Getting started is simple. Visit Afrimercato and click 'Become a Vendor' or 'Partner With Us'. Fill in your details – your name, email, and create a secure password.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Next, set up your store profile. Give your store a memorable name, choose your category, and write a short description that tells customers what makes you special. Don't forget to upload your logo and a cover image – great visuals attract more customers!

<break time="1s"/>

Once you submit your profile, our team will review your application. This usually takes 24 to 48 hours. You'll receive an email when you're approved. While you wait, you can prepare your product information!

<break time="1.5s"/>

Once approved, welcome to your Vendor Dashboard – your command center for success! At a glance, you can see your performance score, total revenue, orders, and customer count. These cards update in real-time as your business grows.

<break time="0.5s"/>

The sidebar is your navigation hub. From here, you can access Dashboard for your overview, Products to manage your inventory, Orders to fulfill customer requests, Reports for detailed analytics, Subscription for your plan settings, and Settings to update your store profile.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Scroll down to see your performance charts. The revenue trend shows your earnings over time. The orders chart breaks down your order statuses. And the category chart shows which products are performing best.

<break time="1.5s"/>

Now let's add your first product! Click on 'Products' in the sidebar, then click the green 'Add Product' button in the top right corner.

<break time="0.5s"/>

A form will appear. Start by entering your product name – be clear and descriptive. Add a description that highlights quality and features. Select your category from the dropdown – this helps customers find your products.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Set your price and choose the unit – per piece, per kilogram, per bunch, or per litre. Enter your current stock quantity. Then upload up to 5 high-quality product images. Tip: the first image is your main thumbnail, so make it count!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Click 'Create Product' and you're done! Your product is now live and customers can find it. You'll see it appear in your products list where you can edit, update stock, or delete it anytime.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Pro tip: Need to update multiple products at once? Select them using the checkboxes and click 'Bulk Actions' to update prices, stock, or delete multiple items in one go.

<break time="1.5s"/>

When customers order from your store, you'll find them in the Orders section. Each order shows the customer's details, items ordered, total amount, and current status.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Orders flow through several stages. When a new order arrives, it's 'Pending'. Once confirmed, it moves to 'Preparing'. Our picker team will pack the order, then a rider will deliver it to the customer. You'll see each status update in real-time.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Click on any order to see the full details. You can view what the customer ordered, their delivery address, and update the order status as needed. Clear communication builds customer trust!

<break time="1s"/>

Track your business growth in the Reports section. View detailed revenue reports, order history, and customer trends. You can filter by date range and export your data for accounting purposes.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Need to update your store details? Visit Settings to change your store name, description, images, or contact information. Keep your profile fresh and up-to-date!

<break time="1.5s"/>

And that's it! You're ready to start selling on Afrimercato. Add your products, fulfill orders with care, and watch your business grow. We're here to support you every step of the way.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Questions? Visit our Help Center or contact our support team anytime. Welcome to the Afrimercato family – let's grow together!
```

### Customer Video - Complete Script for ElevenLabs

```
Craving fresh, quality groceries delivered right to your door? Welcome to Afrimercato – your local marketplace connecting you with the best vendors in your area. Shopping for fresh food has never been easier!

<break time="0.5s"/>

In this quick guide, we'll show you how to find stores near you, browse products, add items to your cart, and complete your first order. Let's get started!

<break time="1s"/>

First, let's create your account. Click 'Sign Up' and enter your name, email address, and create a password. It only takes a minute!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Once registered, you can update your profile anytime. Add your phone number and delivery address to speed up checkout later.

<break time="1s"/>

Afrimercato finds stores near you automatically! On the home page, you'll see featured vendors and stores in your area. Want to explore more? Click 'Browse Stores'.

<break time="0.5s"/>

The Stores page shows all available vendors. Each store card displays the store name, category, customer rating, and distance from you. Looking for something specific? Use the search bar or filter by category!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Click on any store to enter their virtual shop. You'll see the store's profile at the top and all their products below. Each vendor manages their own inventory, so you're always getting fresh items directly from them.

<break time="1s"/>

Want to browse all products across all stores? Click 'Products' in the navigation. You can search by name, filter by category, and sort by price or popularity.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Each product card shows you everything you need – the product image, name, price, and unit. See something you like? Click 'Add to Cart' to add it to your basket!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Click on any product for the full details. Here you can read the description, see more images, check which vendor sells it, and read customer reviews. Adjust the quantity using the plus and minus buttons, then add to cart.

<break time="0.5s"/>

When you add an item, you'll see it fly to your cart! The cart icon shows how many items you have. Keep shopping and add as many items as you need.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Ready to review your order? Click the cart icon. You'll see all your items listed with prices and quantities. Need to adjust? Use the plus and minus buttons. Want to remove something? Click the trash icon. When you're ready, click 'Proceed to Checkout'!

<break time="1s"/>

Checkout has three easy steps. First, enter your delivery address. Fill in your name, phone number, and full address. You can also add special instructions – like 'Ring the doorbell twice' or 'Leave at the back door'.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Next, enter your payment details. We use secure Stripe payment processing to keep your information safe. You can save your card for faster checkout next time. Check the order summary on the right to see your items, subtotal, and delivery fee.

<break time="0.5s"/>

Finally, review everything before placing your order. Check your delivery address, payment method, and order items. If everything looks good, click 'Place Order' to complete your purchase!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Congratulations! Your order is confirmed! You'll see your order number and estimated delivery time. Click 'Track Order' to follow your delivery in real-time.

<break time="1s"/>

Track your order every step of the way! The timeline shows your order's current status – from confirmed, to being prepared, packed, and out for delivery. On the map, you can see exactly where your rider is in real-time!

<break time="0.5s"/>

When your order arrives, the status updates to 'Delivered'. You can rate your experience and leave a review for the vendor and rider. Your feedback helps the community!

<break time="0.5s"/>

View all your past orders in the Orders section. You can reorder your favorites with just one click, making repeat shopping super easy!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Found something you love but not ready to buy? Add it to your Wishlist! And check your Notifications to stay updated on order status, promotions, and more.

<break time="1s"/>

And that's how easy it is to shop on Afrimercato! Fresh, local products from trusted vendors, delivered straight to your door. Happy shopping!

<break time="0.5s"/>

Start shopping today at Afrimercato. For help, visit our support center or contact us anytime. We're here to make your shopping experience delightful!
```

## DESCRIPT WORKFLOW

### For Easy Script-Based Editing

```
1. Import your screen recordings
2. Paste voiceover script into Descript
3. Descript transcribes and aligns audio
4. Edit by editing the text (delete words = delete video)
5. Add:
   - Captions (auto-generated)
   - Shapes for callouts
   - Text titles
6. Apply "Studio Sound" for audio cleanup
7. Export video + SRT captions file
```
