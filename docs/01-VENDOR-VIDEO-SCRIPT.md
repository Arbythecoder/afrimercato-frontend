## VIDEO PRODUCTION NOTES

### Transitions
- Use smooth slide/fade transitions between sections
- Highlight callouts should pop in with a subtle bounce animation
- Use cursor animations to show clicks and interactions

### Color Palette
- Primary: Green (#22C55E) – for buttons, highlights
- Secondary: Yellow/Gold (#EAB308) – for accents
- Background: Light gray/white (#F9FAFB)
- Text: Dark gray (#111827)

### Icons & Illustrations
- Use Lucide React icons (matching the app)
- Animated illustrations for concepts (delivery, success, etc.)

### Music & Sound
- Background: Upbeat, modern African-inspired music (soft)
- Sound effects: Subtle clicks, success chimes, whoosh for transitions
