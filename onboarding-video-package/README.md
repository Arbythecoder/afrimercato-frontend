## Quick Start Guide

### Step 1: Gather Assets (Day 1)
1. Review [06-UI-REFERENCE.md](./06-UI-REFERENCE.md) for screenshot list
2. Capture all required screenshots from the Afrimercato app
3. Download the Afrimercato logo (PNG with transparency)
4. Note your brand colors: Green (#22C55E), Gold (#EAB308)

### Step 2: Generate Voiceover (Day 1-2)
1. Go to [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io)
2. Copy the full script from [05-AI-PROMPTS.md](./05-AI-PROMPTS.md)
3. Choose a voice (Rachel or Adam recommended)
4. Generate and download MP3/WAV files

### Step 3: Choose Your Method (Day 2-3)

**Option A: Budget-Friendly (Free - $25)**
- Use CapCut (free) for editing
- Import screenshots + voiceover
- Add text overlays and transitions manually

**Option B: Professional ($50-75/month)**
- Use Arcade for screen demos with auto-callouts
- Combine with voiceover in CapCut Pro
- Add professional captions with VEED.io

**Option C: Premium All-in-One ($100+)**
- Use Synthesia for AI avatar presentation
- Upload screenshots as backgrounds
- Generate complete video in one platform

### Step 4: Assemble & Edit (Day 3-5)
1. Follow the timeline in [03-STORYBOARDS.md](./03-STORYBOARDS.md)
2. Match visuals to voiceover timing
3. Add callout graphics and highlights
4. Add background music (10-15% volume)
5. Generate auto-captions

### Step 5: Export & Publish
- Export at 1080p, MP4, H.264
- Upload to YouTube, website, or training platform

## Brand Guidelines

### Colors
```
Primary Green:    #22C55E
Secondary Gold:   #EAB308
Background:       #F9FAFB
Text Dark:        #111827
Text Light:       #6B7280
Error Red:        #EF4444
Info Blue:        #3B82F6
```

### Typography
- **Headings:** Inter Bold
- **Body:** Inter Regular
- **Buttons:** Inter Medium

### Tone of Voice
- Friendly and welcoming
- Clear and concise
- Encouraging, not condescending
- Professional but approachable
- Avoid technical jargon
